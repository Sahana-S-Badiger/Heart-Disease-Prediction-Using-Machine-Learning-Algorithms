About Us

Welcome to the Heart Disease Prediction Portal! Our mission is to provide an accessible and user-friendly platform that leverages data science and machine learning to predict the risk of heart disease.

Our portal uses a variety of risk factors including age, sex, chest pain type, resting blood pressure, cholesterol levels, fasting blood sugar, resting ECG, maximum heart rate, exercise-induced angina, oldpeak, and the slope of the peak exercise ST segment to assess the likelihood of heart disease. This prediction tool is designed to aid healthcare professionals and individuals in making informed decisions about their health.

We believe in the power of technology to improve healthcare outcomes and empower individuals to take charge of their health. Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement and staying at the forefront of medical research and technological advancements.

Thank you for choosing our Heart Disease Prediction Portal. Your health is our priority.
